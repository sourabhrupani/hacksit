HackSIT v1.0

HackSIT v1.0 is a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including designers will collabrate intensively to work on software projects.


The IT Association of Saffrony has filtered out problems which affects smooth functioning of college. These problems are categorized into Institute Welfare Problems and Student Welfare Problems.


During 30 Hours of HackSIT v1.0, Teams will work around a particular problem to find out the best feasible and scalable solution.


Hereby, Saffrony is pleased to announce it's First Edition of Hackathon "HackSIT v1.0" on 9th and 10th of March 2018.
