Frequently Asked Questions
  • Who can participate?

    Everyone is welcomed to apply, the only requirement is that you need to be a valid student of Saffrony Institute of Technology!!


  • Is individual participation allowed?

    No! HackSIT v1.0 is strictly a team competition. The maximum number of students in a team is four!!


  • How does the application process works?

    After successful submission of your proposed idea, The core team will go through the idea and depending upon the scalability and the practical approach of your idea, your team will a confirmation mail including a Hack Ticket!!


  • Once Selected, What are things I need to bring?

    Laptop, Hack Ticket and will power to survive the hours of coding!!


  • How much does it cost?

    Application to HackSIT is free of cost!!


  • What do I get by attending HackSIT?

    From networking to meeting experts, prizes to swags and certificates, HackSIT has it all!!
